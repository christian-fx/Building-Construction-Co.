Our Story: Building Dreams Into Reality

Founded in 1985 by John Richardson, Building Construction Co. started as a small family business with a single truck and a big dream. Today, we're a multi-generational company that has grown to become one of the most trusted names in the construction industry.

Our journey began with residential renovations and has evolved into comprehensive construction services spanning commercial developments, industrial complexes, and innovative architectural projects. Through economic shifts and industry changes, our commitment to quality has remained unwavering.
